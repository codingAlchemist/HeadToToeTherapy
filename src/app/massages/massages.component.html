Thai Massage

A Thai massage is a different kind of massage that originated in Thailand over 1000 years ago. It combines broad and targeted acupressure, stimulation and manipulation of energy lines, and assisted yoga postures, which are all enhanced when the client is fully relaxed and breathing deeply. During this treatment, the practitioner creates a dance of movements on the recipient’s body using her thumbs, palms, forearms, elbows, knees and feet, which will open up joints, stretch tendons, tone internal organs and balance energy flow throughout the body. It is performed on a mat and it is recommended to always wear comfortable clothing when receiving a Thai Massage. In Thai Massage, the body is compressed, pulled, stretched and rocked in order to clear energy blockages and relieve tension bottled up in the body. The overall effect is a feeling of deep relaxation, rejuvenation, and heightened states of physical, mental and spiritual well-being, which can often be exciting and powerful to the min/body healing experience.
